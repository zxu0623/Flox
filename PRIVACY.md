# Flox Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

## Summary

Flox does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## Data Storage

Flox uses Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`) to save:
- Workspace names, colors, and URL matching rules you create
- Tab-to-workspace assignments
- Stashed tab sessions (URLs and titles)
- Your preferences (language, idle threshold settings)

This data is stored entirely on your local device and is never sent to any server.

## Data Collection

Flox does **not** collect:
- Browsing history
- Personal information
- Analytics or usage data
- Cookies or tracking identifiers

## Third-Party Services

Flox does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Permissions

Flox requests browser permissions (`tabs`, `storage`, `tabGroups`, `contextMenus`, `alarms`) solely to provide its core tab management functionality. No data accessed through these permissions is transmitted externally.
