Flox — Smart Tab Manager for Focused Work. Your tabs, finally organized. Flox automatically groups your browser tabs by task, shows you what's forgotten, and lets you save and restore entire workspaces in one click.

## Highlights
- 🧠 Auto-classify tabs into task workspaces with customizable URL rules.
- 📦 Stash and restore full workspace sessions, including single-page restore.
- ⏰ Detect idle tabs and quickly clean up what you no longer need.
- 🧭 Manage everything from both Popup and full Dashboard views.
- ⚡ Context menus, shortcuts, drag-and-drop, and real-time sync for fast workflows.

## Privacy
All data is stored locally in your browser. Flox does not collect or upload your browsing data.
